## üéØ What This Strategy Does

Alt22 uses the **Parabolic SAR indicator** for entries and exits:

- **Entry:** When price crosses above Parabolic SAR
- **Exit:** When price crosses below Parabolic SAR
- **Fast-moving:** High frequency, momentum-chasing
- **Trend-following:** Captures explosive moves

### The Parabolic SAR:
Dots that follow price, accelerating with momentum:
- **Dots below price** = Uptrend (long signal)
- **Dots above price** = Downtrend (exit signal)
- **Acceleration factor** increases as trend continues

### High Churn Strategy:
Expect 15-25 trades per year (vs 5-10 for other strategies). More commissions but catches momentum moves.

## üî¨ Parabolic SAR Logic

### How the Indicator Works:

**SAR (Stop and Reverse):**
```
Initial AF (Acceleration Factor) = 0.02
Maximum AF = 0.20
AF increases by 0.02 each period new extreme reached
```

**Uptrend:**
```
SAR dots below price
AF accelerates as trend continues
Reversal when price touches/crosses SAR
```

**Downtrend:**
```
SAR dots above price (exit signal for longs)
AF resets on reversal
Re-enter when price crosses back above SAR
```

### Example Trade (QQQ):

1. **Day 1:** Price $400, SAR $390 ‚Üí Long signal
2. **Day 1-30:** SAR trails below price ($392, $394, $396...)
3. **Day 31:** Price drops to $405, SAR at $402 ‚Üí Exit signal
4. **Result:** 30-day momentum trade captured, exit on reversal
